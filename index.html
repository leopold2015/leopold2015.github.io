Working overtime and conpensation

Word:

compensation: 薪资待遇

quarterly budget: 季度预算

swamped：特别忙、被淹没

crunch time：紧要关头

put together：组织、筹备

overtime: 加班

catch up: 聚会、叙旧

Dialog:

A: Hey Sandra, do you want to grab a drink tommorrow night?

B: Oh sorry, Tony. I have to put in some overtime(加班) tommorrow.

A: Really? Didn’t you work overtime a couple of days ago?

B: I did, but we’re really swamped.

A: Wow, is this a normal requirement for your job?

B: Just at crunch time. Everyone has to put in extra hours.

A: What are you working on?

B: I have to put together the quarterly budget.

A: Yikes! That’s a huge responsibility!

B: I’m pretty used to it now.

A: Do you at least get compensation for overtime?

B: Yeah, overtime is time and a half and a day off in lieu.

A: Sweet! Then it’s totally worth it!

B: It really is. Once I’m finished, we’ll catch up.
